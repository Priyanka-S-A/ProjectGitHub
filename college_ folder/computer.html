Welcome to the Computer Lab at the University College of Engineering 
This facility is designed to support students in developing practical computing skills and working on engineering projects. The lab features high-performance workstations equipped with the latest software and tools, supporting a range of programming languages and engineering applications. With secure, high-speed internet access, students can easily conduct research, collaborate with peers, and access online resources. A wide software suite, including compilers and Integrated Development Environments (IDEs), is available to facilitate coding, simulation, and design work. Students are required to sign in upon entry and sign out upon leaving, ensuring accountability and lab security. Open lab hours vary, so check the schedule, especially around exam times when the lab tends to be busier. Remember to respect the equipment by avoiding food and drink near workstations, handling devices carefully, and reporting any technical issues immediately to lab staff. The Computer Lab at the University College of Engineering is a vital part of the academic experience, providing a hands-on learning environment where students can apply theoretical knowledge gained in lectures. The lab is equipped with modern, high-performance computers capable of running complex engineering software, simulations.
